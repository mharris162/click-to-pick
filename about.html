Welcome to Click to Pick

Who We Are

Click to Pick is committed to helping our neurodivergent friends pick a place to eat.

Click to Pick was founded in 2024. Since then, Click to Pick has helped our friends who are divergent search and filter restaurants to provide specific restaurant recommendation and relieve stress and anxiety when choosing a where to eat.

What We Do

We provide a specific restaurant recommendations based on your preferences- zip code, food preferences, price range, and driving distance. We take the stress out of picking where to eat, all you have to do is click. You click, we pick. You are just a few clicks away from your next bite. What are you waiting for?

Our Story

Throughout my life, I have known many people who have analysis paralysis and have a hard time choosing where to eat. I saw a need for a tool that would help people choose where to eat based on several preferences. So Click to Pick was born. We have gladly helped our neurodivergent friends choose where to eat since 2024.
